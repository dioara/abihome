About Us

Learn about our journey, values, and commitment to excellence in construction.

Our Story

Established in 2018, Abihomes Ltd was founded with a clear vision: to create exceptional spaces that combine innovative design with superior craftsmanship and durability.

Our founder, with over 20 years of experience in the construction industry, recognized a gap in the market for a construction company that truly prioritized quality and longevity in every project. This vision has guided our growth from a small team working on residential renovations to a comprehensive construction company handling everything from bespoke luxury homes to multi-unit developments.

Today, Abihomes Ltd is recognized as a leader in high-quality construction across the Greater Manchester area, with our flagship Omega Apartments development showcasing our commitment to excellence on a larger scale.
