Premium New Build

Construction of a new 5-bedroom luxury residence valued at £750,000, featuring high-end finishes and exceptional attention to detail.

Project Overview

This premium new build project showcases our ability to create exceptional residential properties from the ground up. Located in a sought-after area of Cheshire, this 5-bedroom luxury home combines contemporary design with traditional craftsmanship to create a residence of outstanding quality and enduring value.

Every aspect of the design and construction was carefully considered to create a home that offers both aesthetic appeal and practical functionality, with high-end finishes and exceptional attention to detail throughout.

Key Features

  • 5 bedrooms (3 with en-suite bathrooms)
  • Open-plan kitchen/dining/family room
  • Formal living room and separate dining room
  • Home office/study
  • Media/entertainment room
  • Double garage with utility area
  • Landscaped gardens with patio and outdoor kitchen
  • Energy-efficient design with smart home technology

Construction Process

Design & Planning

Working closely with the client and our architectural partners, we developed a comprehensive design that balanced aesthetic aspirations with practical requirements.

  • Client consultation and brief development
  • Architectural design and planning
  • 3D visualizations and virtual walkthroughs
  • Planning permission and building regulations approval

Groundworks & Structure

The foundation of quality construction begins with meticulous groundwork and structural integrity, ensuring the home will stand the test of time.

  • Site preparation and excavation
  • Foundation construction
  • Structural framework
  • Roof construction
  • External envelope completion

Interior Construction

The interior spaces were crafted with precision and attention to detail, creating a perfect canvas for the high-end finishes to follow.

  • Internal walls and partitions
  • First and second fix electrical and plumbing
  • Underfloor heating installation
  • Plastering and preparation
  • Smart home wiring and systems

Finishing & Landscaping

The final phase brought the project to life with luxury finishes, bespoke elements, and carefully designed outdoor spaces.

  • Bespoke kitchen and bathroom installations
  • Premium flooring and tiling
  • Custom joinery and cabinetry
  • Decorating and final finishes
  • Garden landscaping and outdoor features
